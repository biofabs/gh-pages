Robotics <> Point-of-Care

Time is of the essence when carrying context, robotics in the form of hardware and software is helping gain the appropriate context for physicians, as well as providing a means to maintain optimal compliance with the patient's treatement

BLOOD SAMPLING

Time of diagnosis and appropriate treatment course is tightly linked - robotics helps us get closer to real-time.  In addition to this, there are many reasons to sample blood more frequently as to capture new epigenomic markers that can lead to early detection of disease

LAB TESTS AT HOME

Convenience of lab tests at home, not only provides an easier way to more constant monitoring in chronic disease, but also adds a layer of privacy and in some cases lower costs to the patient
